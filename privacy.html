Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

1. Website Purpose

chapter322.github.io/FreeTapWater is a non-commercial project created to help visitors find restaurants and cafes in the Netherlands that offer fairly priced tap water. The site displays publicly available information about establishments' water policies.

2. Data Collection Practices

Our website:

  • Does not use analytics/tracking cookies
  • Does not use Google Analytics or similar services
  • Uses localStorage to cache restaurant data for 24 hours to improve performance
  • Requests location access only when you click "Find Near Me" (never stored)

3. Types of Data Processed

Restaurant Information

The displayed data may include:

  • Business names and addresses
  • Geographic coordinates
  • Water policy classifications
  • User-submitted comments

Note: For sole proprietorships, this may constitute personal data under GDPR.

User Data

We do not collect:

  • Names or contact information (unless voluntarily submitted via form)
  • Payment information
  • Persistent identifiers

We implement these security measures:
