Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

TL;DR: We don't track users. Restaurant data is public. Owners can soon verify their listings.

1. Website Purpose

fairtapwater.com is a non-commercial project helping visitors find restaurants in the Netherlands that offer fairly priced tap water.

2. Data Collection Practices

  • No analytics/tracking cookies used
  • localStorage caches restaurant data for 24 hours
  • Geolocation used only when clicking "Find Near Me"
  • Form submissions via YouForm (no account required)

3. Data Types Processed

Restaurant Information

  • Business names and addresses (publicly available)
  • Geographic coordinates
  • Water policy classifications:
    • Free - No charge
    • Reasonable - ≤ €1
    • High price - Bottled only
  • User-submitted comments (publicly visible)

Data Verification (Planned Feature)

Coming in 2026:

  • Owner verification:
    • Restaurant owners can claim listings via email verification
    • Verified status will be displayed publicly
    • Owners can dispute inaccurate information
  • Verification process:
    1. Owner submits request via contact form
    2. We verify business registration
    3. Owner receives verification link via official business email

Form Submissions

Via YouForm:

  • Collected:
    • Establishment details (name, address)
    • Water policy information
    • Optional experience comments
  • Never collected:
    • Submitter personal information
    • Payment details
    • Browser fingerprints

5. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Regular security reviews of third-party services
  • 24-hour auto-clear of cached data
  • No storage of sensitive personal data

6. Your Rights

Contact contact@fairtapwater.com to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your submissions
  • Opt-out of processing
For restaurant owners: To verify or correct your listing, email us from your official business address with:
  1. Restaurant name and address
  2. Proof of ownership (KVK extract or similar)
  3. Requested changes (if applicable)

7. Policy Changes

We will notify users of significant changes via:

  • Notice on our homepage
  • Update to this policy's "Last updated" date
